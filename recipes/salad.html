Aunt Myrna’s Party Cheese Salad

About

Another guaranteed hit from Aunt Myrna. Everything but the kitchen sink.

Ingredients

  • 2 small or 1 large Lemon or lime Jell-O
  • Medium size can crushed pineapple
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 green bell pepper chopped
  • 1 small jar of pimento
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 8 oz Cool Whip (Other whipped toppings are available)
  • 1/2 cup nuts – Chopped
  • 1/2 – 3/4 cup shredded american cheese

Steps

  1. Dissolve Jello in Pinapple in sauce pan (low heat)
  2. Add chopped up Cream cheese stir until melted.
  3. Add bell pepper, celery & pimento with nuts. mix.
  4. Add whipped topping
  5. Mix all ingredients and pour in 9×12 glass casserole dish
  6. Sprinkle with cheese
  7. Refrigerate for 4 hours
